Tools

What we have done

We have used our Microsoft Teams group and a collaborative GitHub repository to organise our Project and assessment tasks. We have created a GitHub pages website attached to our GitHub repository. We have also used software applications; image editing software (paint.net, Krita), video editing software (iMovie), Microsoft Office 365, voice recording software, code editors (Unity, VS Code, Pico-8).

Comments on our activity logs

While most of our GitHub commits relate to our website, we have also used the repository to store prototypes, video files and general artifacts. The members who created those artifacts are not necessarily the ones who committed those files as some were uploaded to teams and moved across. Our Teams group and our meeting artifacts give further context to the work that individual team members have done.
