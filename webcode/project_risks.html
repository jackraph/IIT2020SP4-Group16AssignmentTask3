Risks

The game we have chosen to develop is fairly simple in scope. We have created paper prototypes to gain a solid understanding of our requirements. We have mitigated risk from software being unworkable by creating prototypes in multiple IDEs.

The most risky feature would be the networking, which is quite complicated if you have never coded networking before. It’s also possible that the visual art and music that we create will be terrible, in which case our game will look very simple. It’s technically not a problem, but could affect interest in our game, so I feel more time should be given to that as none of us are artists.
