Skills and Jobs

Programmer (online multiplayer)

Role Summary:

The Programmer (Online Multiplayer) is responsible for designing and leading the creation of networking code for the game.

Key responsibilities:

  • Design and document the game’s networking code
  • Lead the team in developing the networking code
  • Design test plans to test the networking code

Other Duties:

  • Supporting the team with other programming duties as needed

Required qualities:

  • Strong knowledge of networking models and protocols
  • Experience coding networked games
  • Communication
  • Organisation
  • Problem solving

Digital Artist

Role Summary:

The Digital Artist is responsible for leading the art direction of the game and delivering visual assets that create an immersive gaming experience.

Key responsibilities:

  • Develop and document the artistic vision for the game
  • Lead the team in developing visual game assets
  • Provide feedback to team to encourage the growth of team members’ artistic skill sets

Required qualities:

  • Strong knowledge of design principles
  • Communication
  • Organisation

Desirable competencies

  • Experience developing games or digital experiences
  • Knowledge of Unity
  • Knowledge of 3D modelling

Community Manager

Role Summary:

The Community Manager is responsible for fostering community around our game.

Key responsibilities:

  • Monitoring and responding to communications on
    • Social networks (Twitter, Twitch, YouTube, Facebook)
    • External Email
    • Game publishing sites (itch.io, indieDB, Steam)
  • Develop and plan social media campaigns aligned with game development milestones
  • Provide community feedback to the development team

Other Duties:

  • Advise personnel on brand identity where appropriate

Required qualities:

  • Awareness of brand
  • Professional use of the written English language
  • Familiarity with online forms of written English language
  • Copywriting
  • Awareness of social media trends and memes
  • Awareness around design principles
  • Public speaking
  • Adaptability
  • Curiosity

Desirable competencies

  • Familiarity with social media management platforms such as Hootsuit.
  • Storytelling
  • Graphic design

Administration Officer

Role Summary:

The Administration Officer is responsible for the day to day organisation of the office.

Key responsibilities:

  • Triage incoming communications
  • Monitor cashflow
  • Manage accounts payable
  • Process payroll
  • Compile reports
  • Minutes taking

Other Duties:

  • Ad hoc office duties

Required qualities:

  • Strong communication
  • Organisation and time management
  • Professional written and verbal communication
  • Attention to detail
  • Multitasking
  • Familiarity with Excel functions, data manipulation and comparison

Desirable competencies

  • Familiarity with MYOB
